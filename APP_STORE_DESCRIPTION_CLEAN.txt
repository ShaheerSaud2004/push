Khafī (خفي) - The Islamic Hidden Word Game

A fun party game for Muslims! Play the classic hidden word game with Islamic categories and find the imposter among your friends.

HOW TO PLAY
1. Set up your game: Choose 3-20 players and select categories
2. Pass the phone: Each player reveals their card secretly
3. Play: Give clues or answer questions about the secret word
4. Vote: Find the imposter who doesn't know the word!
