    Description: Popular moments and events from Islamic history
    [LOCKED - PREMIUM]
================================================================================

   - Muslims capture Jerusalem under Caliph Umar
Battle of Yarmouk
   - Major victory over Byzantine Empire in Syria
Fall of Constantinople
   - Ottoman conquest of Byzantine capital, now Istanbul
Golden Age of Baghdad
   - Center of learning, science, and culture during Abbasid era
Al-Andalus
   - Muslim-ruled Spain known for its achievements
Crusades
   - Series of religious wars between Muslims and Christians
Building of Al-Azhar
   - Ancient mosque and university in Cairo, Egypt
First Hijrah to Abyssinia
   - Early Muslims seek refuge in Ethiopia
Mongol Invasion
   - Mongol armies attack Muslim lands, sack Baghdad
Reconquista
   - Christian reconquest of Spain from Muslims
Ottoman Empire Rise
   - Turkish empire expands and becomes powerful
Salahuddin Ayyubi
   - Great Muslim leader who recaptured Jerusalem
Umayyad Caliphate
   - First major Islamic dynasty after Rashidun
Abbasid Revolution
   - Overthrow of Umayyads, golden age begins
Conquest of Spain
   - Muslims enter and establish rule in Iberia
Siege of Vienna
   - Ottoman attempt to capture Vienna, Austria
Moorish Spain
   - Period of Muslim cultural flourishing in Spain
Age of Discovery
   - Muslim explorers travel and map new lands
