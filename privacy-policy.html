Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Khafī ("Muslim Imposter") ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Player Names: Names you enter for game participants (stored locally on your device)
  • Phone Numbers: Phone numbers you provide when choosing to receive game cards via SMS (only used for sending game cards you request)
  • Game Settings: Your game preferences, custom categories, and difficulty selections (stored locally on your device)

2.2 Location Information

  • Location Data: We only access your location when you explicitly enable location-based categories (e.g., nearby coffee shops, restaurants)
  • Permission Required: Location access requires your explicit permission and is only used to find nearby businesses for game categories
  • No Tracking: We do not track your location over time or share it with third parties

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version (for app functionality)
  • Usage Data: Game results and statistics (stored locally on your device)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game functionality
  • To send game cards via SMS when you explicitly request this feature
  • To generate location-based game categories when you enable location services
  • To improve and personalize your gaming experience
  • To store your game preferences and custom categories locally

4. SMS Messaging

Opt-In Required: We only send SMS messages when you explicitly choose the "Send via SMS" option and provide a phone number during game setup.

  • Messages are sent only for the specific game you request
  • We do not send marketing or promotional messages
  • You can opt-out at any time by replying "STOP" to any message
  • Message and data rates may apply (standard carrier rates)
  • For help, reply "HELP" to any message

5. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Most data (player names, game settings, custom categories) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal information on our servers
  • Phone Numbers: Phone numbers are only used to send requested SMS messages and are not stored permanently

6. Third-Party Services

  • Twilio: We use Twilio to send SMS messages. Twilio's privacy policy applies to their services.
  • Google Places API: We use Google Places API to find nearby businesses when you enable location-based categories. Google's privacy policy applies.

7. Location Services

  • Location access is optional and only used when you enable location-based categories
  • We use location data solely to find nearby businesses for game categories
  • Location data is not stored or shared with third parties
  • You can disable location services at any time in your device settings

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your stored data within the app settings
  • Deletion: You can clear all app data by uninstalling the app or using the "Clear Data" option in settings
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of SMS messages by replying "STOP"
  • Location: You can disable location services at any time

10. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
