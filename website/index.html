How It Works

1

Setup

Choose number of players, imposters, and categories. Add player names for a personal touch.

2

Reveal

Each player taps their card to see their role and word. Imposters see "IMPOSTER" instead of the secret word.

3

Play

Give clues or answer questions. Debate and discuss. Vote out who you think is the imposter.

4

Reveal

See who was right. Celebrate wins and laugh about wrong guesses. Play again!

Pass & Play Design

One phone, everyone plays. Encourages real face-to-face interaction instead of everyone on their own device.

Islamic Categories

Over 20 categories covering Prophets, Seerah, Islamic History, Ramadan, Halal Foods, Masjid Life, and much more. All content is respectful and family-friendly.

Location-Based

Custom Categories

Three Game Modes
